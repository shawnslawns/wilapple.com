Kitchen Tips

01
Salt Your Pasta Water Generously
It should taste like mild seawater. This is your only chance to season the pasta itself.
02
Rest Your Meat Before Cutting
At least 5–10 minutes. The juices redistribute and you'll lose far less on the cutting board.
03
Mise en Place Saves Everything
Prep and measure all ingredients before you start cooking. Professional kitchens swear by it.
04
Acid Brightens Any Dish
A squeeze of lemon or a splash of vinegar at the end lifts and sharpens flavours that taste flat.
